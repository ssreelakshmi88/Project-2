Instructions:

  1. The quizzes consists of questions carefully designed to help you self-assess your comprehension of the information presented on the topics covered in the module. No data will be collected on the website regarding your responses or how many times you take the quiz.
  2. Each question in the quiz is of multiple-choice format.
  3. There is only one possible answer on every question.
  4. Read each question carefully, and click on the button next to your response
  5. Each correct or incoorect response will result in appropriate feedback immediately.
  6. After responding to a question, click on "Next" button to go to the next question.
  7. The total score for the quiz is based on your responses to all questions.
  8. After completion of the quiz score will be displayed on the screen.
  9. When you are completing the quiz make sure you are in a quite environment so you can concentrate well.
  10. GOOD LUCK!!
