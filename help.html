Instructions:

  1. The questionnaire assesses your general knowledge.
  2. You can choose your response from four multiple choices.
  3. Each question has only one correct answer and a correct response is immediately highlighted in green. Otherwise the response will be highlighted in red.
  4. Responding to a question will automatically take you to the next question.
  5. In case, you want to skip a question click “Skip” button.
  6. A progress bar keeps a track on the number of questions played.
  7. The total score for the quiz is based on the number of correct responses.
  8. After completion, the score will be displayed on the screen.
  9. In case, you want to check the correct answers click “Check Answers” in the score section.
